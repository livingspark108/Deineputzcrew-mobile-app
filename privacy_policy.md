# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 06/01/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how **LivingSpark Global Tech Pvt Ltd** ("we", "our", "us"), the developer and operator of **DeinePutzCrew**, collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the **DeinePutzCrew iOS application** published on the **Apple App Store**, and related services provided via [https://deineputzcrew.de](https://deineputzcrew.de).

This app is a **workforce management and attendance monitoring application**. It allows administrators to create and assign tasks, and employees to check in at registered work locations, track working time, upload verification photos, and check out after task completion.

This policy is written to comply with **Apple App Store Review Guidelines**, **GDPR (EU)**, and **German data protection standards**.

---

## 1. App Purpose

The DeinePutzCrew iOS app is used exclusively for:

• Managing employee tasks at predefined work locations
• Verifying employee presence via location check-in
• Recording work time (clock-in / clock-out)
• Uploading selfies and work photos for task verification

The app is intended for **professional use only**.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

• Full name
• Email address
• Phone number
• Employee/User ID
• Login credentials (securely encrypted)

### 2.2 Location Data

• GPS location during **check-in and check-out only**
• Location is used to verify presence at assigned work locations
• **No background location tracking** is performed

### 2.3 Photos & Media

• Selfie photo at check-in (attendance verification)
• Work photos uploaded before check-out (task validation)
• Photos are never used for marketing or advertising

### 2.4 Work & Usage Data

• Task details and status
• Check-in and check-out timestamps
• Work duration
• App usage logs and error logs

### 2.5 Device Information

• Device type
• iOS version
• App version

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data strictly to:

• Confirm employee presence at registered job locations
• Track working hours and attendance
• Verify task completion
• Allow administrators to manage workforce operations
• Ensure system security and prevent misuse

We **do not** use data for:

• Advertising
• Marketing
• Analytics across other apps
• Profiling or automated decision-making

---

## 4. Legal Basis (GDPR – EU)

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

• **Performance of a contract** (employment-related duties)
• **Legitimate business interest** (attendance and task verification)
• **User consent** (camera and location permissions)
• **Legal obligations** where applicable

---

## 5. Apple-Specific Privacy Practices

In compliance with Apple's privacy requirements:

• Location and camera permissions are requested **only when required**
• Permissions are clearly explained before use
• The app does **not** track users across apps or websites
• App Tracking Transparency (ATT) is **not used** because no tracking occurs
• Data is not shared with third parties for advertising purposes

---

## 6. Data Storage & Security

• All application servers and databases are hosted in **Germany** using **DigitalOcean** infrastructure

• No production data is stored outside Germany unless legally required

• All data is transmitted using secure encryption (HTTPS/SSL)

• Data is stored on secured servers with restricted access

• Only authorized administrators can access employee data

• Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied

---

## 7. Data Retention

• Attendance and task data is retained according to company and legal requirements
• Photos may be deleted after internal verification or defined retention periods
• Data is not kept longer than necessary

---

## 8. Data Sharing

We do **not sell or rent** personal data.

Data may be shared only:

• With authorized company administrators
• With trusted service providers (hosting, cloud storage) under confidentiality agreements
• When legally required by authorities

---
