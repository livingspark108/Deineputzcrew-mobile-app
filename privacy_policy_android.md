# Privacy Policy – Android (Google Play Store)

**Effective Date:** 6 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **LivingSpark Global Tech Pvt Ltd** ("we", "our", "us"), the developer and operator of **DeinePutzCrew**, collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the **DeinePutzCrew Android application** published on the **Google Play Store**, and related services provided via [https://deineputzcrew.de](https://deineputzcrew.de).

The DeinePutzCrew Android app is a **workforce management and employee attendance application**. It enables administrators to create and assign tasks, and employees to check in at registered work locations, record working hours, upload verification photos, and check out after completing assigned work.

This policy is written to comply with **Google Play Developer Program Policies**, **GDPR (EU)**, and **German data protection regulations**.

---

## 1. App Purpose

The DeinePutzCrew Android app is used exclusively for:

• Assigning and managing employee tasks
• Verifying employee presence at predefined work locations
• Recording working time (clock-in and clock-out)
• Collecting selfies and work photos for attendance and task verification

The app is intended strictly for **professional and organizational use**.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

• Full name
• Email address
• Phone number
• Employee / User ID
• Login credentials (stored in encrypted form)

### 2.2 Location Information

• GPS location during **check-in and check-out only**
• Location data is used to confirm presence at assigned job locations
• **No continuous or background location tracking** is performed

### 2.3 Photos & Media

• Selfie photo during check-in for identity and attendance verification
• Work photos uploaded before check-out as proof of task completion
• Photos are used strictly for internal operational purposes

### 2.4 Work & Usage Data

• Task details and task status
• Check-in and check-out timestamps
• Total working duration
• App usage logs and technical error logs

### 2.5 Device Information

• Device model
• Android OS version
• App version

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used only to:

• Verify employee attendance at registered work locations
• Track working hours and task completion
• Enable administrators to monitor workforce operations
• Improve app stability, security, and performance

We **do not** use personal data for:

• Advertising or marketing
• Selling or renting data
• Cross-app or cross-service tracking
• Automated profiling

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data based on:

• Performance of contractual and employment-related obligations
• Legitimate business interests (attendance and workforce management)
• User consent for location and camera access
• Legal obligations where applicable

---

## 5. Google Play–Specific Privacy Disclosures

In accordance with Google Play policies:

• Location access is limited to work-related check-in and check-out
• Camera access is used only for selfies and work photo uploads
• The app does not request unnecessary or unrelated permissions
• No personal data is shared with third parties for advertising purposes

---

## 6. Data Storage & Security

• All servers and databases are hosted in **Germany** using **DigitalOcean** infrastructure
• Data is transmitted using secure encryption (HTTPS / SSL)
• Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only
• Appropriate technical and organizational security measures are in place

---

## 7. Data Retention

• Attendance, task, and time-tracking records are retained according to company and legal requirements
• Photos may be deleted after verification or defined retention periods
• Data is not stored longer than necessary for its intended purpose
